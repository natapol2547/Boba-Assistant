ISE celebrates 12th anniversary with new management policy to move towards international standards The International School of Engineering (ISE), Chulalongkorn University is recognized internationally as the most preferred international engineering school in ASEAN that cultivates world-class engineers who are well-equipped with required skills and knowledge and be ready for local and global challenges. Today is the day to celebrate its 12th anniversary and success with the announcement of new management policy to enhance education quality at par with international standards, as well as to highlight its leadership as the hub for engineering education in the ASEAN region. In the past 12 years, the International School of Engineering has cultivated 1,266 potential engineering graduates who work in both local and international companies. Assoc. Prof. Tawatchai
